Short Bio

I am a final year undergrdauate in Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna (IITP), India. I am broadly interested in research in Theoretical Computer Science and Communication Theory. I have been extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Amitalok J. Budkuley (IIT Kharagpur), Prof. Sidharth Jaggi (University of Bristol & CUHK), Prof. Laura Luzzi (ENSEA, CNRS), and Prof. Preetam Kumar (IIT Patna) in my undergraduate research.

I also love playing outdoor sports specially Cricket and Volleyball in my free time.
