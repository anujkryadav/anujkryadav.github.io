Short Bio

I am a final year undergrdauate in Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Patna (IITP), India. I am broadly interested in research in Theoretical Computer Science and Communication Theory. I have been extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sidharth Jaggi (University of Bristol), Prof. Amitalok J. Budkuley (IIT Kharagpur), Prof. Laura Luzzi (ENSEA, CNRS), and Prof. Preetam Kumar (IIT Patna) in my undergraduate research.
